# Waitlist Nurture Sequence

## Email 1 - Immediate Confirmation
Purpose: Confirm signup and reinforce the mission.

## Email 2 - Community Vision
Send: 3-5 days later
Message: Explain how Connect My Tribe helps people find events and build stronger in-person community in Chicago.

## Email 3 - Early Access Push
Send: 1-2 weeks before launch
Message: Invite waitlist members to be early testers, share the app with friends, and look out for launch-day updates.

## Success Metric
Number of waitlist signups generated before launch.
