# Campaign Launch Checklist

## Before You Share
- Confirm the page copy matches your current product positioning.
- If your app has separate sign-in and sign-up URLs, replace the shared root URL with those exact links.
- Decide which teammate owns posting and which teammate owns follow-up.

## First Push
- Share the landing page with friends in Chicago.
- Send the page to 2-3 businesses that may want to host events.
- Use the teaser posts to drive people to the landing page, then into the live app.

## Track
- Clicks from the landing page into the live app
- Number of new account creations
- Number of returning sign-ins
- Which post drove the most visits or responses

## Convert
- Watch where users drop off after landing on the page.
- Refine CTA copy if people click through less than expected.
- If needed, move the page into the main app for an even smoother entry flow.
