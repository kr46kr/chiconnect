# Landing Page Brief

## Goal
Give Connect My Tribe a clean front door that explains the product and sends real users directly into the live app to sign in or create an account.

## Primary Audience
- Individuals in Chicago looking to make new connections
- Friends, classmates, and early users who need a simple entry point into the app
- Local businesses and community hosts who may want to explore the platform

## Hero Section
**Headline:** Find your people in Chicago.

**Subheadline:** Connect My Tribe helps people discover local events, meet others with shared interests, and build real community around them.

**Primary CTA:** Sign In to the App

**Secondary CTA:** Create an Account

## User Flow
1. A visitor lands on the page.
2. They understand what Connect My Tribe does and who it is for.
3. They choose whether to sign in or create an account.
4. They are sent into the live app.

## Trust Signals
- Built for people in Chicago looking to make real connections
- Personalized recommendations based on user preferences
- Designed around in-person community, not endless scrolling

## Suggested Page Sections
1. Problem: It is hard to find the right events and people in a big city.
2. Solution: Connect My Tribe recommends relevant events and communities based on user interests.
3. How It Works: Create a profile, sign in, discover events and groups, build real connections.
4. Why It Is Different: More personal than event-listing apps and more community-focused than social feeds.
5. Entry CTA: Send users directly into the live app.
